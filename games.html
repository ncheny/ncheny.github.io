Companion

I worked closely with the digital marketing specialist and the development coordinator in creating and updating graphics for the Gallery. During the term, I was given the task to create graphics for the different programs, exhibitions, and galas that the Gallery would be hosting, as well as promotional ads for the Gallery that were displayed in the Georgia Straight, the Cultch, online Google Ads, Gallery's social media accounts, and other magazines. In addition to digital medias, I also designed physical posters were displayed during annual gala, vinyl signs, rack cards at conventions, and entrance digital displays used in the gallery.

Each project usually have different size requirement that the Gallery's printing or publishing partner would have required which I would to communicate with regularly to check up on file status or to resolve issues that have arised.

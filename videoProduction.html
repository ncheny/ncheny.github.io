Video Production

Individual and Arts& Health Collaboration
Over the course of IAT 344 I created an individual commerical video promoting local fresh produce via the use of chromakeying and animated motion graphics. Our team also created a short 5 minute documentary made in collaboration with "Vancouver Board of Parks and Recreation" and "Vancouver Coastal Health".
  • TASKS Ideation, sketching, researching, motion graphics, sound editing
  • TOOLS After Effect, Premiere Pro, Audacity, Photoshop
  • INDIVIDUAL & GROUP
  • Spring 2018 4 Months

[Individual Commerical Video] [Arts and Health Documentary]

Individual Commercial

The individual video is a 30 seconds commercial promoting the idea of buying and eating locally grown produce. In this project, the elements where I chose to focus on are chromakeying, motion graphics animation,color grading, usage of masks, and cutting to music.Chromakeying were applied to the bowl of fruits on top of a greenscreen fabric. Then they are color graded to achieve better contrast and brightness for a high key, colorful palette.
Throughout the video, each change of frame was timed to each beat of the background music. In different sequence, I picked different motion graphics animation to emphasize the mood of the season. Small plants sprouted from the ground in Spring, water rippled out in Summer, morphing blobs changes colors in Autumn, and snow fell in the Winter sequence.

In the last section of the video, I decided to play with video masks. Initially, I tried moving the masks around, but the effect was disorienting and was difficult to match to the music tempo, so it was kept stationary in the end.

Arts and Health Documentary

My role in the project mostly consisted of production assistance and sound recording. During the process of filming, I had to rent and set up the lavalier mic and zoom recorder and make sure everything sounded great. While there were some equipment hiccups along the way, once all the filming was done, I worked on cleaning some of the audio files and syncing those that are needed in Audacity and Premiere Pro for our team editor.

In addition to sound, I helped prepare the documentation needed for our participants and recorded our process. Near the end when my work was handed over to the our team editor, I worked on our final documentary poster in Photoshop. The poster image was selected fom our footage and was designed to fit into the official style of the Arts and Health program.
